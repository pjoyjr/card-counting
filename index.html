Card Counting Game

Welcome to the Card Counting Game! Here's how to play:

  • Select the number of decks you want to use.
  • Set the card flipping speed.
  • Click "Start" to begin the game.
  • Try to keep track of the count of the cards.
  • You will be prompted to guess the count every 6-15 cards and once at 80% of deck usage.

The count is calculated as follows:

  • Cards 2 through 6 increase the count by 1.
  • Cards 10, Jack, Queen, King, and Ace decrease the count by 1.
  • Cards 7, 8, and 9 do not affect the count.

The true count is calculated by dividing the running count by the number of decks remaining.
